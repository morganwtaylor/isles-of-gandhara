# Session 2: DM Notes
---

## Session Summary

New player Paul joined the party at the docks. The ship escaped Kell's Landing after being boarded and searched (Tessa talked them through). Six days of downtime sailing toward Joybright. Stopped at Thornhaven where they encountered Marren Brightwell (Tessa's husband) and his companion Karsa. Tavern encounter at the Golden Chain went sideways when the bard Nessandrle attempted magical manipulation. Murdock broke the spell by stealing the lute. Session ended with party scattered, Karsa and Nessandrle both down in the back room, and Tonyult watching.
