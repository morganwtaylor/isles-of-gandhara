## NPCs Introduced

### Tessa Brightwell

- Human woman, early 40s, matriarch of the ship
- Sailed from Chult with her son and ~30 followers, seeking Joybright
- Former Lliiran lay worshiper; husband Marren arrested 10 years ago for possessing Book of Exultation
- Reaction to execution: went silent and unresponsive, later composed herself
- _Party relationship: POSITIVE - they saved her people_

### Corvin Ashdale

- Half-elf dockworker/smuggler, late 20s
- Gave Lil Moon cargo jobs for silver/gold
- Runs packages for the Dissenter Network (Mask/Tymora faithful)
- _Party relationship: NEUTRAL - potential hook into resistance_

### Vigilant Corus Thane (DECEASED)

- Helmite officer, human, ambitious and corrupt
- Stole Wolfric's coin purse in the market
- Other Helmites disliked him; they watched but didn't intervene
- Killed 3 on the ship before party killed him
- _Note: His death may not be mourned by fellow Helmites, but the Accord will still seek justice_

### Broken Tower Keeper (unnamed)

- Runs underground Ilmater sanctuary
- Initially suspicious, won over by Murdock's oath

## Consequences & Open Threads

### Immediate Consequences

**Wanted:** The party killed a Helmite Vigilant. Even if other Helmites disliked Thane, the Accord will respond. Expect investigation, possible warrants.

**Witnesses:** The other Helmites watched the fight. They know what the party looks like. Did any of them report it?

**The Ship:** 30 Lliiran followers now depend on the party. Where do they go? Joybright is gone. Can they stay in Kell's Landing?

**Murdock's Oath:** He is now bound to Ilmater. What does this mean mechanically? Roleplay implications?

### Unresolved Plot Threads

**The Lastbloom:** A scarlet flower appeared somewhere in Kell's Landing at the moment of Lliira's death. The party hasn't found it yet.

**Corvin's Network:** The smuggler offered Lil Moon work. Is there more? Connection to the Dissenter Network?

**Marren Brightwell:** Tessa's husband was arrested 10 years ago. Is he still alive? Could he be rescued?

**The Broken Tower:** Underground tunnel network revealed. What else is down there? Who else uses it?

## Player Character Notes

**Wolfric:** Has been in Kell's Landing 8 months. Born in Autumn Vale with 12 siblings. Raised in Ilmater church. Contact at Broken Tower.

**Murdock:** From Summervale. Separated from family by Helmites at young age. Trained at monastery, doesn't follow gods—until now. Swore Oath of Ilmater this session.

**Lil Moon:** ~40 years old. Born under pitch black sky until moon appeared. Wants to unite neighboring tribes. New to Kell's Landing.
