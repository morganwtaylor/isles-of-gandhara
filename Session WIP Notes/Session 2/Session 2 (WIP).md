We start in city of Kells landing
Our new player Paul is sneaking through the city and ends up at the docks and links with the party. 
The party leaves get questioned by guard ships and boarded and searched. Tessa convinces them of that everything is in order and party heads off to continue on to Joybright.
We begin sailing and have several days of down time where the party learns of other shipmates a bit and get some rest.
worlfric and murdock pray to illmater and feel empowered. 

After about 6 days the ship makes a stop at a small working town called Thornhaven. It lies at the edge of highgland planes south of the capital of veltharion

They meet Karsa, a strong, tall scary soldier. And with him Marren. We learn little of why they travel together or their past but Marren Brightwell learns of his wifes arrival on the ship and runs to meet her.

The party leaves to go to tavern. When there they struck up conversation with the bar tender and a less than friendly goblin. Across from the Goblin sits an aasimar named seraph. Seraph attempts to judge Wolfright, which causes tension and wolfright confronts the man. During this time the bard playing in the tavern slowly begins to influence the party. Murdock decides to put a stop to this and takes the lute and runs. Once the music stops the party realizes the manipulation and run. Except Karsa who intends to kill the bard. After some strife the bard and Karsa end up alone in a back office both very injured. 

Tonyult, proprietor of Golden chain tavern sites there as well. Looking interested to see the results of this fight. After a quick exchange it appears both Karsa and nessandrle (the bard) fall.

The party is left split running through the city and back to the ship. Unsure of what they will find.
