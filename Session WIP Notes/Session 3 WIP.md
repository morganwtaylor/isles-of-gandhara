the party goes beneath the deck and confront little moon.  He seems confused about where he is at (missed last session). They talk about what is happening. Wolfric mentions the aasimar, and the judging.  They discuss what to do as marren is set to sail to joybright.

Marren nearly convinces them to set sail in the morning. They begin to settle in.

Karsa gets a messager from tonyult th at arrives to the ship. The contract for his life is due, he must kill Marren in 3 days.

Down below Wolfric nods off in the main quarters and listens to marren and tessa read the book he holds close. Above deck Murdock strums the bards lute. Water walt and lil moon discuss to each other about lives and background. THey also cover that karsa has to kill marren and what that might bring.

Eventually all nod off as the night takes over....

In the morning some discussion happens but the ship soon is underway. Sailing to Joybright.

As they are sailing not half a day a helmite cutter stops them at the seas. Tessa tries to usher those from Kells landing and marren below deck but not all choose to go. The leader of the vessel boards their ship asking for papers. Then after a brief look demands a search. Wolfric makes clear move to prepare for battle and the fight begins. Clashing of swords and spells unleash on the decks. Crossbow bolts come shooting over. The party gets hurts, wolfric goes down but they stablize with the immense healing offered. Marren is dragged from below deck by Karsa, and in fear he reads from the book and a pillar of light strikes many on both sides. The party turns on him as the helmite guards thin out. Marren in a final act, tries to restore Wolfric far beyond what is needed. Before the final guard is struck down by him. There is no rest though as Marren is also finished off by Karsa quickly.

Two ships now float side by side... one without a crew, the other with a crew thats broken.
