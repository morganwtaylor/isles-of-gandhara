_Wondrous item, artifact (requires attunement)_

A single scarlet flower that never wilts. Warm to the touch. Holding it brings the echo of a laugh, the ghost of a smile.

---

## Properties

**Indestructible.** Cannot be destroyed by mundane means. Fire doesn't burn it. Blades pass through it.

**Hope's Beacon.** Glows faintly (dim light, 5 ft) near someone who genuinely _needs_ hope.

**Joy's Refuge.** You can't be magically compelled to feel despair or hopelessness.

**Memory of Joy.** Once per long rest, touch a willing creature to let them experience a vivid memory of their happiest moment. No mechanical effect, but can calm panic, break despair, or shift resolve.

---

## Dormant

_The flower responds to how it is carried. Acts of genuine joy, hope, and compassion feed it. Cruelty and cynicism cause it to fade._

_There is more here. You can feel it. But embers need fuel to grow._

---

_It doesn't demand heroism. It doesn't demand victory. It only asks that the wielder, in whatever small way they can, keeps joy alive._
