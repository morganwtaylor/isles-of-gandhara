## Passive Properties (Always Active)

- **Indestructible.** Cannot be destroyed by mundane means
- **Lliira's Warmth.** Immune to magical despair, hopelessness, or sorrow effects. You still feel natural grief — the Book prevents _weaponized_ despair
- **Joyful Presence.** Creatures within 10 ft who are friendly/neutral feel at ease. Advantage on Persuasion checks to comfort, inspire, or calm
- **Anti-Divination.** Cannot be targeted by divination magic from servants of Bane, Helm, or Tyr unless caster succeeds DC 20 ability check (spellcasting ability)
- **Loyalty.** If lost or stolen, the Book returns within 24 hours. Cannot be prevented except by antimagic field, a deity's direct intervention, or willing relinquishment
- **Divine Authorship.** Detect magic reveals overwhelming Evocation aura. Any caster recognizes this as divine — written by a goddess, not a mortal

## Active Features

### Words of Comfort

**Action** | **Range:** 5 ft | **Uses:** Proficiency bonus / long rest Read a passage to a creature. It regains **1d8 + CHA mod** hit points.

### Festival's Memory

**1 minute** | **Uses:** 1 / long rest Read aloud. Up to **6 creatures** who can hear you gain the benefits of a **short rest** (spend Hit Dice, recover features) without actually resting.

### Hymn of Exultation

**Action** | **Duration:** 10 minutes | **Uses:** 1 / long rest You and all allies within **30 ft** gain:

- Temporary HP = **your level + CHA mod**
- Advantage on saves vs. charmed, frightened, or possessed
- Area fills with golden light and distant festival music
- Evil-aligned creatures in area have **disadvantage on Concentration checks**

### Unbind

**Action** | **Range:** Touch | **Uses:** 2 / long rest End one condition on a creature: **charmed, frightened, paralyzed, or stunned.** The target hears a warm, defiant laugh and knows — for one moment — what it feels like to be truly free.

### Joy is Contagious

**Passive trigger** — whenever you restore HP using the Book's spells or features, one additional creature within **10 ft** of the target regains HP = **CHA mod.**

### The Last Dance ★

**1-minute ritual** | **Range:** 60 ft, 40 ft radius | **Uses:** 1 / long rest Lliira's ultimate gift:

- All **allies** in area regain **6d8 + CHA mod** HP
- All **allies** freed from any curse, disease, poison, blindness, or deafness
- All **hostile creatures** in area: **WIS save vs. spell DC** or **stunned 1 round** and **frightened 1 minute** (they witness a vision of Lliira's last festival — thousands dancing, refusing to kneel)
- For **1 hour** after: area is consecrated ground. Undead and fiends have disadvantage on attacks within it. Scarlet flowers bloom in cracks and corners

### Goddess's Chosen ★

- **CHA score +2** (max 24)
- **Advantage** on all CHA saving throws
- **Death Defiance:** When reduced to 0 HP, use **reaction** to drop to 1 HP instead. Each creature of your choice within 20 ft regains HP = CHA mod. **1 / long rest**

### Lliira's Voice ★

_Sending_ at will — but only messages of hope, comfort, or encouragement. Recipient hears your voice layered with something older, warmer, laughing. They are **immune to magical fear and despair for 24 hours.**

## Joybright Convergence Effects

_Active only while the Book is within Joybright_

- **Evocation aura fills the entire building** the wielder stands in
- **The Book has a heartbeat.** Hot to the touch but not painful. The wielder feels it through the leather

### In the Sunken Temple (with Lastbloom present)

The full Convergence triggers. The Unwritten Chapter fills with Lliira's final words. The Echo distributes into the artifacts, the stones, and every person present.

**After the Convergence — The Open Book:**

- The Book **cannot be closed.** Pages always display whichever passage the nearest person most needs to read
- New words appear in the Unwritten Chapter — but they are **Paul's** now, not Lliira's. The Book records his acts of joy, defiance, and compassion in divine script
- **Joybright becomes a permanent sanctuary** while the Open Book remains in the city:
    - Accord divination cannot penetrate city limits
    - All creatures in the city have advantage on saves vs. fear, charm, and despair
    - Genuine celebration causes permanent scarlet flowers to bloom
    - The Weeping Watch garrison can no longer comfortably remain

---

## Sorrowsworn Interaction

The Book is the natural counter to grief-spawned creatures:

- **Genuine joy within 30 ft** of a Sorrowsworn gives it **disadvantage on all attacks and saves**
- Reading from the Book, using Hymn of Exultation, or triggering The Last Dance all count as generating genuine joy
- The Sorrowsworn doesn't just take damage from this — it **unmakes.** Joy doesn't kill grief. It transforms it
